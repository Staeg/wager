Heroes for each faction. They use the standard HP/Damage/Range/Actions/Value notation, but the Value is usually irrelevant.

Artificer

   * 16/6/2/2/20

   * Endturn Random Strike 3/R: at the end of turn, deal 3 damage to a random enemy within attack range
