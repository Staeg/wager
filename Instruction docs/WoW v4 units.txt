Custodian
* Page: 3/1/1/2
* Librarian: 2/0/3/12
   * Sunder 1: at the end of turn, reduce the Armor of a random enemy in range by 1
* Steward: 20/3/1/10
* Gatekeeper: 32/4/2/25
   * Aura 2 - Undying 2: if an ally within 2 hexes would die and has >0 attack damage, it loses 2 attack damage instead of taking damage
Weaver
* Apprentice: 8/1/2/5
   * Push 1: moves the target back 1 hex horizontally if possible after attacking
* Conduit: 5/2/3/10
   * Aura 1 - Amplify 1: passively increases the values of ally abilities by 1 for all allies within 1 hex
      * Does not Amplify other Amplify effects or negative effects (such as Armor -X) and drawbacks (such as Charge Y)
* Seeker: 3/1/4/10
   * Ramp 1: increases attack damage by 1 after successful attacks
* Savant: 6/4/4/25
   * Splash 1: deals 1 damage to all enemies adjacent to attack target after attacking
Artificer
* Tincan: 11/2/1/6
* Golem: 16/2/1/14
   * Armor 2: reduces all damage taken by 2
* Kitboy: 6/2/2/10
   * Repair 1: at the end of turn, heal all adjacent allies by 1
* Artillery: 8/4/4/25
   * Followup 2/6: at the end of turn, additionally deal 2 damage to a random enemy within 6 range
Purifier
* Penitent: 5/1/1/5
   * Rage 1: gain 1 attack damage after taking damage
* Priest: 3/1/3/10
   * Heal 1: at the end of turn, heal random ally in range for 1
* Avenger: 20/3/1/12
   * Vengeance 1: whenever an adjacent ally dies, gain 1 attack damage
* Herald: 6/1/4/25
   * Charge 3 - Summon 2 Blades: Every 3 turns summons 2 Blades
      * Blade: 1/2/1/1 unit, created adjacent to its summoner exhausted
