Upgrades
Custodian
* T1:
   * Frenzy: Pages gain the ability “Onhit Self Ramp 1”, which lets them increase attack damage by 1 after successful attacks
* T2:
   * Sweeping sands: Random Sunder abilities become Area Sunder, which hit all enemies within range instead of randomly
* T3:
   * Trespassers: Stewards gain the ability “Wounded Self Ramp 1”, which lets them gain 1 attack damage after taking damage
Weaver
* T1:
   * Skirmish tactics: Apprentices gain the “Onhit Retreat 1” ability, which lets them move backwards after successfully attacking
* T2:
   * Deep freeze: Whenever an enemy is successfully affected by a Freeze effect, deal 5 damage to them.
* T3:
   * Farcasting: All units gain +1 range
Artificer
* T1:
   * Corrosion: Tincans gain the “Onhit Target Sunder 1” ability, which lets them reduce the Armor of a random enemy in range by 1 at the end of turn
* T2:
   * Armor kits: Kitboys gain the ability “Passive Aura 1 Armor 1”, which passively increases the armor for all allies within 1 hex by 1
* T3:
   * Carpet bombing: Random Strike abilities become Charge 2 Area Strike abilities, which hit all enemies within range instead of randomly, but occur half as often
Purifier
* T1:
   * Alacrity: Blades are now created adjacent to the highest health ally in range instead of the summoner, and they are ready instead of exhausted
* T2:
   * Salvation: Random Heal abilities become Area Heal abilities, which target all allies within range instead of randomly
* T3:
   * Bladestorm: “Charge 3 Turnstart Summon 2 Blades” abilities become “Turnstart Summon 2 Blades” abilities, which causes them to trigger every turn instead of every 3 turns
